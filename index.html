Why use this tool?

The 20-20-20 rule helps reduce Digital Eye strain and keep your eyes healthy. Acorrding to the American Academy of Ophthamology, humans normally blink 15 times per minute, but studies show that can be reduced to as little as 5 times in a minute when using digital devices.


Use this timer to ensure that you're giving your eyes regular breaks.
